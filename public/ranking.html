The Best Movies According to Anna-Maria

My top 3 movies of all-time

Pan's Labyrinth

A political movie dressed as a fairytale. Actually, it is a cinematic masterpiece against fascism, both horrifying and visually appealing

The Color of Pomegranates

Symbolic, philosophical, visually bombastic, it narrates the life of the poet Sayat Nova, as perceived by the director.

Whisper of the Heart

The purest love story between two kids with great aspirations and dreams. I'm not crying, you are crying.
